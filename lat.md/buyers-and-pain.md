# Buyers and pain

Confidence statistics, pain themes, consultant economics, personas, triggers, industry selling notes, and the episodic-vs-continuous tension.

## Confidence–execution gap

**92%** prioritize redesign; **11%** feel confident executing—**81-point** deficit (Deloitte HC Trends 2021, via devnotes). Core buyer emotion for [[odaas-core#ODaaS product thesis]].

## Pain themes (condensed)

Six clusters from research—diagnosis, data, disruption, alignment, consulting dependency, turnover/knowledge.

1. **When/how to restructure** — M&A, leadership, strategy, AI, decay.
2. **Data-poor decisions** — Rare ONA and employee involvement — [[strategic-gaps#ONA and informal organization]].
3. **Cost, speed, disruption** — Calendar drag, productivity dips.
4. **Strategy–structure** — Resource waste.
5. **Consultant reliance** — [[buyers-and-pain#Consultant reliance]].
6. **Knowledge loss** — Post-reorg attrition — change quality matters.

## Consultant reliance

MBB engagements **$500K–$2.5M+**; partner rates **$1,100+/hr** benchmarks — pushes buyers to platform + capability — [[strategic-gaps#Market entry vectors]].

## Buying personas

CHRO/CPO own strategy; CEO in smaller firms; COO/CFO/Transformation join large programs. CHROs rank org design / change among top themes — devnotes §6.4.

## Purchase triggers

**Hard:** M&A, leadership, RIF, AI, IPO, regulation. **Soft:** performance, spans, bottlenecks, attrition, pivots. **Frequency:** many firms reorganize every 1–2 years — episodic habit — [[buyers-and-pain#Continuous design gap]].

## Industry notes (selling context)

Vertical nuances for positioning—not feature specs.

- **Technology** — Fast roles, Team Topologies, high WTP.
- **Healthcare** — Silos, margin, compliance drag.
- **Financial services** — Regulation, matrix, budget.
- **Manufacturing** — Industry 4.0, cost, global matrix.

## Continuous design gap

Episodic projects vs standing capability and monitoring—AI outpaces annual planning — [[odaas-core#ODaaS product thesis]], [[strategic-gaps#Continuous vs episodic]].
