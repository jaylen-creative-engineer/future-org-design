# Market and TAM

Market size, growth, timing, and structural context for ODaaS—fragmentation, adjacent segments, and why demand is accelerating now.

## Executive snapshot

Org design is critical but buyers lack confidence. Working TAM ~$6–8B today toward ~$15–25B by 2030; narrow consulting ~$1.63B; broader OD ~$21B. Top 10 share under 5% — [[competition#Consulting market structure]].

## Why now

Five timing forces from research: AI adoption without workflow redesign, consulting economics, fragmented category, prescriptive AI norms in HR, and Team Topologies / SBO driving structural churn.

1. **AI forcing function** — [[ai-and-data-layer#AI and workflow redesign]].
2. **Consulting limits** — [[buyers-and-pain#Consultant reliance]].
3. **Fragmentation** — room for a platform — [[strategic-gaps#Market entry vectors]].
4. **Prescriptive AI** — [[strategic-gaps#Prescriptive analytics gap]].
5. **Team Topologies & SBO** — [[market-and-tam#Team Topologies movement]].

## People analytics and org design

Analytics makes design defensible (spans, attrition, DEI, outcomes). Most tools are not yet prescriptive on **structure** — [[strategic-gaps#Prescriptive analytics gap]].

## Workforce planning disconnect

Strategic WFP (~$2B, ~15% CAGR) and org design are often separate models—unification is the ODaaS integration play.

## Team Topologies movement

Major book adoption and enterprise cases, second edition 2025—yet **no native software** for continuous Team Topologies evolution. Entry vector: [[strategic-gaps#Market entry vectors]].

## HCM context

Org design sits between core HR, talent, WFM, and analytics—today a patchwork of point tools — [[ai-and-data-layer#Emerging AI-augmented stack]].
