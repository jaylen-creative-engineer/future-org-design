# Strategic white space

Ten research-backed gaps, prescriptive analytics, operating model depth, ONA integration, human–AI design, continuous vs episodic, 2×2 positioning, and go-to-market entry vectors.

## Ten critical market gaps

No single vendor closes all of these; together they define ODaaS scope.

1. **Informal + formal** — ONA outside design tools — [[ai-and-data-layer#Organizational network analysis (ONA)]].
2. **AI structural partner** — Proactive vs reactive — [[strategic-gaps#Prescriptive analytics gap]].
3. **Work layer** — Tasks with positions — Beamible-adjacent.
4. **Operating model** — Decision rights, governance, WoW — [[strategic-gaps#Operating model layer]].
5. **Democratized design** — Line managers, governed.
6. **Continuous design** — Closed loop — [[buyers-and-pain#Continuous design gap]].
7. **External labor market** — Skills/salary in the design flow.
8. **Matrix depth** — Multi-dimensional reporting.
9. **Human + AI org design** — [[ai-and-data-layer#Human–AI teaming]].
10. **Change execution** — Last mile past the chart.

See also [[competition#Collective gap (what incumbents miss)]], [[odaas-core#ODaaS product thesis]].
