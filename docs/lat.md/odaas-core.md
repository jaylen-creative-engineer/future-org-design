# ODaaS core concepts

Core definitions for Organization Design as a Service: what we are building, why it wins, and how it connects to market, competition, and the closed-loop chain.

## ODaaS product thesis

AI-first platform replacing episodic consultant-led redesign with continuous, data-driven org design—faster and cheaper than traditional engagements.

The north-star is one intelligence loop: [[closed-loop-value-chain#Closed-loop pipeline]]. Today’s vendors split that loop — see [[competition#Who owns which slice]].

### Confidence deficit (why buyers need this)

Leaders prioritize structural work but rarely feel equipped to execute. Quantified in [[buyers-and-pain#Confidence–execution gap]].

### Collective market gap

No incumbent owns the full AI-centered workflow — [[strategic-gaps#Ten critical market gaps]] and [[competition#Collective gap (what incumbents miss)]].

## Strategic position
